# TalkToBook: product overview

TalkToBook turns a recording you own into a clean, designed ebook your audience can download. Paste a YouTube URL (or upload a transcript), and it builds a formatted EPUB you can use as a lead magnet, course handout, webinar follow-up, or newsletter bonus.

## Who it is for

Creators who already have spoken content and want a keepable artifact from it: podcasters, course creators, coaches, consultants, and anyone running webinars or workshops. It is for repurposing your own work, not for copying other people's.

## How it works

1. Paste a captioned YouTube URL you own, or upload a `.txt`, `.md`, `.srt`, or `.vtt` transcript.
2. TalkToBook pulls the transcript, strips timestamps and speaker markers, and merges fragments into readable paragraphs.
3. You get a free EPUB preview to judge before paying anything.
4. Subscribe to the Creator Plan to download clean editions in EPUB, PDF, and Kindle (AZW3), with the colophon removed and your own cover and accent.

## What you get

- Free preview: a watermarked EPUB with a plain auto-generated cover.
- Creator Plan: unlimited clean editions while active, in EPUB, PDF, and Kindle (AZW3), with the colophon removed and a cover and accent color you choose.

## What it does and does not do

- It cleans and formats spoken prose into proper paragraphs and credits the original speakers.
- It sets interviewer turns apart so a conversation reads like a conversation.
- It does not invent structure your talk did not have, and it does not rewrite your ideas. Light AI restructuring is on the roadmap.
- Built-in transcription for audio-only sources is planned; for now, transcribe audio first, then paste or upload it.

## Use cases

- Lead magnet: put the best workshop notes behind an email form as a downloadable book.
- Course companion: give students a lesson, cohort call, or expert interview they can annotate.
- Webinar follow-up: send attendees a useful takeaway file instead of another replay link.
- Newsletter bonus: package a private conversation or teaching session as a subscriber keeper.
- Back-catalog repurposing: pull evergreen ideas out of old recordings and give them a longer shelf life.

## Rights and acceptable use

You must own or have the rights to anything you upload, and you confirm this before each build. TalkToBook is a tool that reformats a transcript you supply; it does not host or distribute a catalog of third-party works.
