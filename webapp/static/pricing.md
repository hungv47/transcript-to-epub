# TalkToBook: pricing

TalkToBook has a free preview and one paid plan, the Creator Plan, billed monthly or yearly. Prices are in US dollars.

## Free preview

- Price: $0.
- Includes: a clean, readable EPUB with a plain auto-generated cover.
- Watermark: a small "Made with TalkToBook" colophon.
- No account required. Use it to judge the result before paying.

## Creator Plan

Everything in the free preview, plus:

- Unlimited clean editions while the plan is active.
- The colophon removed.
- Your own cover image and accent color.
- Downloads in EPUB, PDF, and Kindle (AZW3) where available.

### Price

- Monthly: $7 per month.
- Yearly: $67 per year, which is about 20% less than paying monthly ($84 per year at the monthly rate).

You choose monthly or yearly at checkout.

## Billing

- The Creator Plan is a subscription processed by our payment provider.
- It unlocks clean editions while active. You can cancel through the billing provider; access continues until the paid period ends.
- Because each deliverable is a digital file generated on demand, past charges are generally final. If something went wrong, contact us and we will make it right.
