Questions.

    

    

      

        Does it read like a real book?
        
Spoken prose stays spoken prose. The engine strips timestamps and merges fragments into paragraphs with proper typography. We won't invent structure your talk didn't have. Light AI restructuring is on the roadmap.

      

      

        Can I use any YouTube video?
        
No — this is for content you own or have rights to. You confirm that before each build. It's a tool for creators repurposing their own work, not for ripping other people's.

      

      

        What if I only have audio?
        
Transcribe it first (most podcast hosts and tools export one), then paste it here. Built-in transcription is the next thing we're working on.

      

      

        What formats do I get?
        
The preview is EPUB. Unlocking adds PDF and Kindle (AZW3) alongside a clean, branded EPUB.
