Questions

    

    

      
Does it read like a real book?
The engine strips timestamps and filler and merges spoken fragments into paragraphs with proper typography. Spoken content is still spoken content — it won't invent structure your talk didn't have. Light AI restructuring is on the roadmap.

      
Can I use any YouTube video?
This is for content you own or have the rights to — you confirm that before generating. It's a tool for creators repurposing their own work, not for ripping other people's videos.

      
I only have audio, not a transcript.
Transcribe it first (most podcast hosts and tools export one), then paste it here. Built-in transcription is the next thing on our list.

      
What formats do I get?
The free preview is EPUB. Unlocking adds PDF and Kindle (AZW3) alongside a clean, branded EPUB.
