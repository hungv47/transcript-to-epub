# TalkToBook: frequently asked questions

## What is TalkToBook?

A tool that turns a recording you own into a clean, designed ebook. Paste a YouTube URL or upload a transcript, and it builds a formatted EPUB you can hand to your audience as a lead magnet, course handout, webinar follow-up, or newsletter bonus.

## Does it read like a real book?

Spoken prose stays spoken prose. The engine strips timestamps and merges fragments into paragraphs with proper typography. It will not invent structure your talk did not have. Light AI restructuring is on the roadmap.

## Can I use any YouTube video?

No. This is for content you own or have the rights to. You confirm that before each build. It is a tool for creators repurposing their own work, not for ripping other people's.

## What if I only have audio?

Transcribe it first (most podcast hosts and tools export a transcript), then paste or upload it. Built-in transcription is the next thing we are working on.

## What formats do I get?

The free preview is an EPUB. The Creator Plan adds PDF and Kindle (AZW3) alongside a clean, branded EPUB.

## How much does it cost?

The preview is free. The Creator Plan is $7 per month or $67 per year (about 20% off paying monthly). See [pricing](/pricing.md) for what each tier includes.

## Can I cancel?

Yes. You cancel through the billing provider, and access continues until the end of the paid period.

## What can I upload?

A captioned YouTube URL, or a `.txt`, `.md`, `.srt`, or `.vtt` transcript file.
